How to Book a Verified Eco Stay

Updated on March 10, 2026 • 4 min read

Sustainable travel starts with making conscious choices about where you stay. At EcoKumari, every "Verified" stay is vetted for its environmental impact and benefit to the local tribal community.

Step 1: Finding Stays

Log in to your dashboard and navigate to the Browse Stays tab. You can filter locations based on eco-impact score, price, and proximity to Kanniyakumari's natural landmarks.

Tip: Use our AI Itinerary Planner to automatically suggest stays that match your sustainable travel goals.

Step 2: Checking Verification

Look for the Verified Eco badge on listing cards. This badge ensures that the host has passed our strict 75-point sustainability audit, which includes waste management and water conservation standards.

Step 3: Secure Payment

Once you've selected your dates, click "Book Now". You can pay securely using Razorpay (UPI, Credit/Debit). All payments are encrypted, and a percentage of the service fee goes directly to the Tribal Artisan Fund.

Warning: Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in may incur a fee of 50% as per our sustainable tourism policy.

FAQs

Can I book on behalf of a large group?

Yes. For groups larger than 10 people, please contact our Institutional Support team to ensure the local community can accommodate the capacity sustainably.
