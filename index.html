The Mission

Integrated Verified Digital Tourism &
Tribal Empowerment Portal for Kanniyakumari

Kanniyakumari, renowned for its rich green landscapes, scenic lakes, cultural heritage, and tribal communities, lacks a dedicated verified digital platform that consolidates authentic tourism information, accommodation booking, and local livelihood opportunities.

This project proposes the development of a government-verified, one-stop digital tourism portal for Kanniyakumari that integrates accommodation booking, local experience discovery, eco-tourism promotion, and a marketplace for tribal products. The platform provides authenticated listings of hotels, homestays, guides, and eco-tour packages, ensuring reliability and transparency for travelers.

The solution incorporates features such as geo-tagged attraction mapping, real-time booking capabilities, verified host badges, and an administrative dashboard for tourism authorities. By combining tourism digitization with community empowerment, the platform aims to enhance visitor experience, strengthen local economic development, and promote sustainable practices.
